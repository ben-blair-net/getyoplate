Get Yo Plate — Demo FAQ

Quick answers before you try the ordering demo. Takes two minutes to read, way less time to have questions about.

⚠️ This is a demo. No real money changes hands and no real order gets sent to Avant's kitchen. Reply "PAID" and everything after it — the payment reminders, the confirmation, the pickup date — is simulated so you can see exactly how the real thing will feel.
Getting Started
→What am I actually testing?
A preview of the ordering system Get Yo Plate will use for weekly drops — instead of DMing Avant on Instagram, you'll fill out a short order form, get a text confirmation, and "pay" (not really, see above). We want your honest reaction to how that feels.
→How do I place a test order?
  • Enter your name and phone number (used only to personalize the demo — nothing is sent anywhere)
  • Pick how many plates you want
  • Add notes or requests if you'd like — totally optional
  • Tap Place order to see your receipt
→What happens after I order?
You'll land on a receipt, then can tap through to a simulated text thread. It walks you through payment instructions, replying "PAID," a short wait while the kitchen "confirms" you, and an optional opt-in for next week's drop list — just like the real flow will work, minus the real payment.
Food & Dietary Info
→What's the "Dietary Alerts" box?
It lists the allergens in that week's dish (pork, dairy, gluten, etc.) so you know what's in your plate before you order. It's a heads-up, not a filter — it won't stop you from ordering something you're allergic to, so please read it.
→Can I request substitutions or changes?
You can leave a note with the order, but Avant can't always accommodate it. Here's how he put it: "Some of this stuff just has to be made in a batch in a big pot like my Grandma taught me. Unfortunately that means I can't always accommodate dietary restrictions — I'll let you know in the dietary alerts what I can't change." — Avant
Feedback
→There's a survey at the end — do I have to fill it out?
No, but it helps a lot. It's three quick questions about how easy the ordering flow felt. Tapping Open feedback form opens a short Google Form in a new tab with your answers already filled in — just review and tap Submit. Nothing goes out until you do, and you can skip it entirely if you're short on time.
Can't find your answer here? Just reply to whoever sent you this link, or use the feedback survey at the end of the demo — happy to answer anything about the food, the flow, or the plan for the real thing.
